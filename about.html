The Team

Technetium Engineering is powered by a team of engineers and PhDs united by a passion for space exploration, autonomous systems, and cutting-edge on-orbit services. Artificial intelligence and machine learning are at the core of our work, deployed not only for spacecraft autonomy, but across a broad range of complex challenges. From real-time 3D reconstruction and visual navigation to predictive analytics, simulation environments, and advanced data modeling, our team leverages AI/ML to accelerate innovation and deliver smart, adaptive systems.

Our mission goes beyond orbit. Whether it's enhancing the autonomy of legacy satellites, developing intelligent ground systems, or creating entirely new frameworks for space operations, we bring AI/ML expertise that bridges research and application. Every project we pursue reflects our vision: to make satellites of yesterday, today, and tomorrow more autonomous, more reliable, and more capable.
